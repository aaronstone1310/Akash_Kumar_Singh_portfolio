I am Akash Kumar Singh, a dedicated researcher in the field of quantum computing. I graduated with a BS-MS in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Tirupati, India, in 2023. During my undergraduate studies, I focused on Quantum Error Correction, specifically designing a scheme to implement CSS codes on Measurement-Based Quantum Computation (MBQC) using ZX Calculus.
Currently, I am pursuing an M.Tech in Quantum Computing at the Defence Institute of Advanced Technology, Pune. My academic journey has fueled my fascination with the mathematics and creativity involved in developing quantum algorithms. I aim to leverage my expertise in Quantum Error Correction and ZX Calculus to design more efficient algorithms for quantum computation.
My research interests lie in the development of quantum algorithms for linear algebra, differential equations, and optimization. I am passionate about advancing the field of quantum computing and contributing to innovative solutions in this rapidly evolving domain.
