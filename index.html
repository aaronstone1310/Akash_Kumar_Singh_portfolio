I am Akash Kumar Singh, a dedicated researcher in the field of quantum computing. I graduated with a BS-MS in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Tirupati, India, in 2023. During my undergraduate studies, I focused on Quantum Error Correction, specifically designing a scheme to implement CSS codes on Measurement-Based Quantum Computation (MBQC) using ZX Calculus.
Currently, I am pursuing an M.Tech in Quantum Computing at the Defence Institute of Advanced Technology, Pune. My research interests include developing quantum algorithms for linear algebra, differential equations, and optimization. Currently, I am focused on Quantum Algorithms for Differential Equations as part of my M.Tech Dissertation. I aim to leverage my expertise in Quantum Error Correction and ZX Calculus to design more efficient algorithms for quantum computation.
